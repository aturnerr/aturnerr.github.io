The Adventures of Squidwod

The Adventures of Squidwod is a 3D game developed in Unity by myself and two other group members for the subject COMP30023. The project tasked us with developing a game which we were given full creative freedom over. Our final submission was a somewhat interesting take on a typical boss fight where the player is required to defeat the giant seagull collect as many "eggs" as possible.
